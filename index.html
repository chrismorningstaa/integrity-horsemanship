The Horseman's Connection

A Magazine Built for Everyday Riders

Founded by horse trainer Randall Shirk and published by IntegriHorse Media, The Horseman's Connection is a bi-monthly print magazine created for riders who want practical, honest horsemanship help they can use every single day.

Each 32-page issue is mailed directly to your home — filled with real-world insight you can put to work in the saddle, in the harness, and in the pasture.

Our Story

Rooted in Character, Integrity & Faith

Randall and Lorelle Shirk are the owners of Integrity Horsemanship and IntegriHorse Media, dedicated to helping people and horses through practical, real-world education.

"We believe in bringing out the beauty of God's horse — and using the horse to illustrate life lessons."

They travel to clinics, events, and communities sharing lessons in horsemanship and leadership — teaching clear, down-to-earth biblical principles that people can live out every day, supported by a wonderful team that believes in the mission.
